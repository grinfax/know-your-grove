A community exploring its past

Know Your Grove is the best (and most fun!) way to explore one of Miami's oldest and most historic neighborhoods: Coconut Grove. This locative media app invites you on a bike ride or walking tour to learn about the history of a once vibrant community struggling under the weight of gentrification.
